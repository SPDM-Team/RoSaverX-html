How to Create a Roblox Group

Creating a Roblox group is a way to connect with your friends and fans. Follow the steps below:

1. Visit the Groups Page

Navigate to the groups page on the Roblox website and click 'Create Group'.

2. Fill in Group Details

Provide a group name, description, and choose an emblem. Make sure the name is unique and represents the purpose of your group.

3. Set Group Entry Settings

Choose if you want the group to be public (anyone can join) or private (users need approval to join).

4. Pay the Group Creation Fee

There's a one-time fee associated with creating a group. Once paid, your group will be created.

5. Manage the Group

Once your group is created, you can invite members, create roles, set permissions, and more. Manage your group through the 'Group Admin' page.

For more detailed information, visit the official Roblox documentation.
